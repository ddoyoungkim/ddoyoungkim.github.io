Welcome to my page! I am a PhD Candidate at the Korea Advanced Institute of Science and Technology (KAIST) in the School of Computing, Daejeon, Korea, supervised by Prof. Jae-Gil Lee. My research focuses on enhancing AI technologies for real-world applications, including continual learning from unstructured and evolving data, practical applications of deep learning, and improving data robustness and efficiency in AI systems. I have gained valuable experience as a Research Scientist intern at Naver AI Lab. My work has been recognized with publications in top conferences such as ICML, AAAI, NeurIPS, and KDD, reflecting my contributions to the field of AI and machine learning. News
